About Us

We are a team of passionate entrepreneurs committed to helping startups succeed. Our mission is to empower the next generation of innovators. The "Rural Entrepreneurship Development Phase 2" project was established based on the decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. PQ-317 of July 13, 2022. This project BASICS FOR ESTABLISHING A BUSINESS INCUBATOR will be implemented in the Republic of Karakalpakstan, Bukhara, Jizzakh, Navoi, Kashkadarya, Samarkand, Syrdarya, Surkhandarya, Tashkent and Khorezm regions during 2022-2026. Business incubator centers were established in 3 cities of Samarkand, Bulungur district and Akdarya district in SAMARKAND region.
